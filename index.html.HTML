Understanding Malaysia’s Financial Literacy Landscape

About Financial Literacy

Financial literacy refers to the ability to understand and manage personal finances effectively. It includes budgeting, saving, investing, debt management and financial planning.

According to the Malaysian Financial Education Network (FEN), financial literacy is important to help Malaysians make wise financial decisions and achieve better financial well-being.

Financial Issues in Malaysia

Debt Problems

Many Malaysians face financial stress due to high debt and poor money management habits.

Low Emergency Savings

Many individuals do not have enough emergency savings to face unexpected financial situations.

Retirement Concerns

Malaysians are becoming increasingly worried about retirement planning and future financial security.

Digital Financial Literacy

Online Banking

Digital banking services make financial transactions easier and faster.

Scam Awareness

Malaysians need stronger awareness about online scams and cybersecurity threats.

Cashless Society

The use of digital payments and e-wallets continues to grow rapidly in Malaysia.
